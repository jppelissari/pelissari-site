## Hero
About

Eight years operating in environments
where the margin for error
was effectively zero.

That background didn’t give me stories. It gave me a model for seeing where systems break under pressure.

## Structure
### Where this came from
I didn’t begin in product. I began in live operations — where coordination failure is immediate, visible, and expensive.

### What operations taught me
Execution can be happening perfectly while the experience still fails. That happens when the system loses legibility.

### What I bring into design
I work on the layer where perception, action, and structure meet — especially in systems where users follow up and operators improvise.

## Proof Strip
Luxury hospitality taught me what invisible failure costs. UX training gave me the tools to model it. The work now is designing systems that stay legible under pressure.
